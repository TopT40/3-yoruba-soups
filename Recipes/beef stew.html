Beef stew

Description

Beef stew is a type of Nigerian soup popularly eaten by the Yoruba tribe of Nigeria. It is made from beef and pepper.

Ingredients

  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • Palm oil
  • Seasoning (stock cubes)
  • Beef
  • Beef stock

Steps

  1. Blend the pepper, onions and tomatoes together
  2. To a cooking pot, add vegtable oil, palm oil and sliced onions then set it on fire
  3. After 2 minutes, add the blended pepper mix and add salt with seasoning cubes
  4. Dilute with beef stock and add already cooked beef to the stew
  5. Leave to cook for about 20 mins
