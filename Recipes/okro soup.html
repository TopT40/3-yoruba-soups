Okro soup

Description

Okro soup is a type of Nigerian soup popularly eaten by the Yoruba tribe of Nigeria. It is made from a farm produce called okro.

Ingredients

  • Okro
  • Salt
  • Locust beans
  • Seasoning (stock cubes)

Steps

  1. Grate the okro and but it your cooking pot
  2. Add water to the greated okro and set it on fire
  3. Add maggi, locust beans and salt to the okro on fire
  4. Stir and allow to cook for about 5 minutes
